Refund Policy

30-Day Guarantee Period

A 30-day money-back guarantee is available on all purchases. If you're not satisfied with Local Password Vault for any reason, contact me within 30 days of your purchase date and I will process a full refund.

How to Request a Refund

  1. Email support@localpasswordvault.com with your order number or the email used for purchase
  2. Request your refund (no explanation required)
  3. Your refund will be processed within 3-5 business days

Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Depending on your bank or credit card company, it may take 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

After the Guarantee Period

After 30 days, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you experience technical issues that prevent you from using the software, please contact support and I will work with you to resolve the issue or provide a refund if a solution cannot be reached.

Free Trial

A free 7-day trial is available to evaluate Local Password Vault before purchasing. The trial includes all features of the paid version, allowing you to fully assess whether the software meets your needs.

License Revocation

If a refund is issued, your license key will be deactivated. You will no longer be able to access premium features, though your existing vault data will remain accessible in read-only mode.

Exceptions

Refunds may be declined if:

  • The request is made more than 30 days after purchase without extenuating circumstances
  • Evidence of license key sharing or abuse is found
  • Multiple refund requests have been made by the same customer

Contact

For refund requests or questions about this policy, contact me at support@localpasswordvault.com. I respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days.
