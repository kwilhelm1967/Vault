Privacy Policy

Your privacy is protected by design, not by policy.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

The Short Version: Local Password Vault is designed so that I have no access to your data. Your vault is stored locally on your device, encrypted with keys I never see. I cannot read, access, or sell your passwords because I never receive them.

Information I Do Not Collect

Because Local Password Vault operates entirely offline on your device, I do not collect:

  • Your passwords or vault contents
  • Your master password or encryption keys
  • Usage data or analytics from the application
  • Device fingerprints or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any telemetry or tracking information

Information I May Collect

The only information I may collect is:

Purchase Information

When you purchase a license, I receive your email address and payment confirmation from our payment processor (Stripe). This information is used solely to deliver your license key and provide purchase-related support.

Support Communications

If you contact me for support, I retain our email correspondence to provide effective assistance and maintain support records. This includes your email address and the content of your messages.

Trial Signups

If you sign up for a free trial, I collect your email address to deliver your trial key and send occasional product updates. You can unsubscribe from product updates at any time.

How Your Data is Protected

Local Password Vault uses AES-256-GCM encryption with Argon2id key derivation. Your vault is encrypted on your device before it's ever saved. The encryption key is derived from your master password, which I never see or store.

Data Sharing

I do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. The only sharing that occurs is:

  • Payment processing through Stripe (for purchases)
  • Email delivery services (for license keys and support)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any email or purchase records I hold
  • Unsubscribe from any email communications
  • Request a copy of any data I have about you

Children's Privacy

Local Password Vault is not intended for children under 13. I do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact me at support@localpasswordvault.com.
