Security and Privacy

Local Password Vault protects your vault with AES-256-GCM encryption. Your master password is processed using Argon2id key derivation with hundreds of thousands of iterations to create your encryption key.

Each vault has a unique salt, making every vault cryptographically independent.

No. I have no access to your vault. Your data is encrypted locally on your device using a key derived from your master password. I cannot see your password, access your encryption keys, or view any of your vault contents.

Important: For security reasons, I cannot recover or reset your vault. Your master password is the only way to decrypt and access your data.

Choose a strong but memorable master password and create encrypted backups regularly.

No. Local Password Vault runs 100% offline. After you download and install the app, everything happens locally on your machine. There are no servers, no telemetry, and no background data sharing.

Using Your Vault

You can export your vault for safekeeping at any time. I recommend maintaining an encrypted backup on a USB drive or external hard drive for optimal data protection.

Because Local Password Vault never touches the cloud, you're in full control of backup and recovery.

If your device is lost, your vault remains secure. All passwords are encrypted with AES-256 and can only be unlocked with your master password.

If you have a backup, you can restore your vault on a new device. If you lose both your device and backups, your data cannot be recovered—that's part of the trade-off for true local privacy.

The Floating Icon gives you quick access to your vault. It stays available on your desktop so you can copy and paste credentials without reopening the main window every time.
