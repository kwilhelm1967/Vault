## üìß Email Delivery Process

### 1. Prepare Email Templates
Use the provided email templates in `license-packages/email-templates/`:
- `single-user-email.html`
- `family-plan-email.html`


### 2. Generate License Keys
Use the license generator in `license-generator/index.html` to create unique license keys for each customer.

### 3. Customize Email Template
- Replace placeholders with customer information
- Insert generated license key(s)
- Update purchase date and order number
- Add your company branding

### 4. Send Purchase Confirmation
- Send email immediately after purchase
- Include download links for appropriate platform(s)
- Attach or link to documentation

## üíø Distribution Methods

### Direct Download
1. Host application files on your website
2. Create download links for each platform:
   - Windows: `LocalPasswordVault-Setup.exe` and `LocalPasswordVault-Portable.exe`
   - macOS: `LocalPasswordVault.dmg`
   - Linux: `LocalPasswordVault.AppImage`
3. Protect downloads with customer email verification

### App Stores (Optional)
- Microsoft Store (Windows)
- Mac App Store (macOS)
- Linux repositories (Ubuntu, etc.)

### Enterprise Deployment
For Business Plan customers:
1. Provide MSI installer for Windows
2. Provide PKG installer for macOS
3. Include deployment scripts and documentation
4. Offer remote installation assistance
