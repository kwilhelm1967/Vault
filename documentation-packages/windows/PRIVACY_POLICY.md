# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2024**

Local Password Vault ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our password management application.

---

## Our Privacy Commitment

**Local Password Vault is designed with privacy as a core principle.** Unlike cloud-based password managers, we:

- ✅ Store all data locally on your device only
- ✅ Never transmit your passwords to any server
- ✅ Never access or view your vault contents
- ✅ Never track your usage or behavior
- ✅ Never sell or share any data

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect, store, or have access to:

- Your master password
- Your stored passwords or account credentials
- Your notes, custom fields, or any vault contents
- Your device information or identifiers
- Your location or IP address
- Your browsing history or app usage patterns
- Any personal information whatsoever

---

## How Your Data is Stored

### Local Storage Only

All vault data is:
- Stored exclusively on your local device
- Encrypted with AES-256-GCM encryption
- Protected by your master password
- Never transmitted over the internet

### Encryption Details

- **Algorithm:** AES-256-GCM
- **Key Derivation:** PBKDF2 with SHA-256
- **Iterations:** 100,000
- **Your master password never leaves your device**

---

## Information We May Collect

### License Activation

When you activate a license, we process:
- License key
- A hardware fingerprint (anonymized device identifier)

This information is used solely to:
- Validate your license
- Prevent unauthorized use
- Limit activations per license

**We do NOT associate license data with any personal information.**

### Website Interactions

If you visit our website (localpasswordvault.com):
- Standard web server logs (IP, browser type, pages visited)
- Cookies for essential website functionality
- Email address if you sign up for trial or support

This data is separate from the application and is never linked to your vault.

### Support Communications

If you contact us for support:
- Email address
- Any information you choose to provide
- Communication history

We use this only to assist you and improve our service.

## Data Security

### Application Security

- Industry-standard AES-256 encryption
- Zero-knowledge architecture
- No cloud storage or transmission
- Automatic memory clearing for sensitive data
- Rate-limited login attempts

### Our Infrastructure

- Secure payment processing via Stripe
- HTTPS-only communications
- Regular security audits

## Third-Party Services

### Payment Processing

We use **Stripe** for payment processing. When you purchase:
- Payment information goes directly to Stripe
- We receive only order confirmation (no card details)
- See Stripe's privacy policy: https://stripe.com/privacy

### Email Services

We use **Brevo** for transactional emails (receipts, trial info):
- Email address and name
- Used only for account-related communications
- See Brevo's privacy policy: https://www.brevo.com/legal/privacypolicy/

### Analytics

**We do NOT use any analytics or tracking in the application.**

Our website may use minimal analytics for:
- Page view counts
- General geographic region
- No personal identification

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access
- All your vault data is on your device - you have full access

### Portability
- Export your data anytime in JSON or CSV format

### Deletion
- Delete your vault data using the "Clear All Data" option
- Request deletion of any support communications

### Opt-Out
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data
- Unsubscribe from marketing emails (if any)

---

## Data Retention

### Application Data
- Stored until you delete it
- Removed completely when you uninstall
- No backups kept by us (we have no access)

### License Data
- Retained while license is active
- Deleted upon request after license expiration

### Support Data
- Retained for support purposes
- Deleted upon request
