Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data really stored only on my device?

Yes. Local Password Vault uses 100% local storage. Your encrypted vault never leaves your computer—no cloud, no servers, no third-party access. We literally cannot see your data.

What if I forget my master password?

Your master password is the only key to your vault. We recommend writing it down and storing it in a safe place. Because we never have access to your password, we cannot reset it for you—that's the security tradeoff that keeps your data truly private.

Can I import from my current password manager?

Absolutely. We support direct imports from LastPass, 1Password, Bitwarden, Dashlane, Keeper, and Chrome. Most imports take less than 30 seconds.

What happens if my computer crashes?

Your vault is stored as an encrypted file on your device. You can back it up like any other file—copy it to an external drive, or use your existing backup solution. The file remains encrypted and useless without your master password.

Why a one-time purchase instead of subscription?

We believe security tools should not hold your data hostage behind recurring payments. Pay once, own it forever. You keep full access to your passwords even if we disappeared tomorrow.
