Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Our Privacy Commitment

Local Password Vault is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • Store all data locally on your device only
  • Never transmit your passwords to any server
  • Never access or view your vault contents
  • Never track your usage or behavior
  • Never sell or share any data

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your master password
  • Your stored passwords or account credentials
  • Your notes, custom fields, or any vault contents
  • Your device information or identifiers
  • Your location or IP address
  • Your browsing history or app usage patterns
  • Any personal information whatsoever

How Your Data is Stored

Local Storage Only

All vault data is:

  • Stored exclusively on your local device
  • Encrypted with AES-256-GCM encryption
  • Protected by your master password
  • Never transmitted over the internet

Encryption Details

  • Algorithm: AES-256-GCM
  • Key Derivation: PBKDF2 with SHA-256
  • Iterations: 100,000
  • Your master password never leaves your device

Information We May Collect

License Activation

When you activate a license, we process:

  • License key
  • A hardware fingerprint (anonymized device identifier)

This information is used solely to validate your license and prevent unauthorized use.

Website Interactions

If you visit our website:

  • Standard web server logs
  • Cookies for essential functionality
  • Email if you sign up for trial or support

Support Communications

If you contact us, we store communication history to assist you.

Third-Party Services

Payment Processing

We use Stripe for payment processing. Payment information goes directly to Stripe - we never see your card details.

Email Services

We use Brevo for transactional emails only (receipts, trial info).

Your Rights

  • Access: All vault data is on your device
  • Portability: Export anytime in JSON or CSV
  • Deletion: Clear All Data option in Settings
  • Opt-Out: Uninstall removes all local data

Summary

Question Answer
Do you see my passwords? No - Zero-knowledge architecture
Is my data in the cloud? No - Local storage only
Do you track me? No - No analytics in the app
Do you sell my data? No - We have no data to sell
Is my data encrypted? Yes - AES-256-GCM
