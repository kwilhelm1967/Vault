Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2025

The Short Version

Local Password Vault is designed from the ground up to protect your privacy. Your passwords and data are stored only on your device, encrypted with keys only you control. We cannot access your data because we never receive it.

1. Our Privacy Commitment

Local Password Vault ("we," "our," or "the App") is built on a fundamental principle: your data belongs to you. Unlike cloud-based password managers, we have designed our application so that:

  • Your passwords never leave your device
  • We have no servers that store your data
  • We cannot access, read, or decrypt your vault
  • We cannot reset your password or recover your data

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your master password
  • Your stored passwords or credentials
  • Your vault contents or metadata
  • Your 2FA/TOTP secrets
  • Your personal notes or secure information
  • Any data you enter into the application

3. Data We May Collect

3.1 License Verification

When you purchase and activate a license, we process:

  • License key (to verify validity)
  • Email address (for purchase receipt and support)
  • A device identifier (to manage license activations)

This information is processed through our payment provider (Stripe) and is used solely for license management.

3.2 Anonymous Analytics (Optional)

With your explicit consent, we may collect anonymous usage statistics such as:

  • App version
  • Operating system
  • Feature usage patterns (no personal data)
  • Crash reports (no vault data included)

You can disable analytics at any time in Settings. Analytics never include your passwords or vault contents.

4. How Your Data Is Protected

  • AES-256-GCM Encryption: Your vault is encrypted using military-grade encryption
  • PBKDF2 Key Derivation: 100,000 iterations protect against brute-force attacks
  • Zero-Knowledge Architecture: Your master password never leaves your device
  • Local Storage Only: All data remains on your computer

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe: Payment processing (see Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Email Provider: For purchase receipts and support communications

These services do not have access to your vault data.

6. Data Retention

Your vault data is stored only on your device. We retain:

  • Purchase records for tax and legal compliance
  • Support communications for quality assurance
  • License activation records for fraud prevention

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your purchase information
  • Request deletion of your license records
  • Opt out of analytics
  • Export your vault data (from within the app)
  • Delete your vault at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Local Password Vault is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date.
